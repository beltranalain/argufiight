# Coin System - User Guide

## Overview
Coins are the virtual currency used in ARGU FIGHT to participate in belt challenges and create tournament belts. This guide explains how to use coins effectively.

---

## How to Get Coins

### 1. Purchase Coins
- **Location:** Click "Buy Coins" in your user dropdown menu (top right)
- **URL:** `/coins/purchase`
- **Packages Available:**
  - **Starter:** $5 = 500 coins (base) + 0 bonus = **500 coins total**
  - **Popular:** $10 = 1,000 coins (base) + 100 bonus = **1,100 coins total**
  - **Large:** $25 = 2,500 coins (base) + 500 bonus = **3,000 coins total**
  - **Mega:** $50 = 5,000 coins (base) + 1,500 bonus = **6,500 coins total**
  - **Ultimate:** $100 = 10,000 coins (base) + 4,000 bonus = **14,000 coins total**

### 2. Win Belt Challenges
- When you win a belt challenge, you earn coins back
- **Reward:** Typically 50-100% of the entry fee (varies by belt value)
- **Consolation:** Even if you lose, you may receive a small consolation prize

### 3. Admin Grants
- Admins can grant coins for special events or promotions
- Check your transaction history to see admin grants

---

## How to Use Coins

### 1. Challenge for a Belt
- **Cost:** 50-500 coins (varies by belt value)
- **How:**
  1. Go to the Belt Room (`/belts/room`)
  2. Browse available belts or view a specific belt
  3. Click "Challenge for Belt"
  4. Pay the entry fee (coins are deducted immediately)
  5. Wait for the belt holder to accept your challenge
  6. If accepted, a debate is created automatically
  7. Win the debate to claim the belt!

### 2. Create Tournament Belts
- **Cost:** 1,000-5,000 coins (varies by tournament tier)
- **How:**
  1. Create or manage a tournament
  2. Select "Add Belt Prize"
  3. Pay the coin cost to create a tournament belt
  4. The belt will be awarded to the tournament winner

### 3. View Your Coin Balance
- **Location:** Top right dropdown menu (shows balance at top)
- **Full Details:** Click "My Coins" to see:
  - Current balance
  - Transaction history
  - All coin activities

## Coin Economics

### Base Rate
- **100 coins = $1 USD**
- All packages follow this base rate
- Bonus coins are added on top

### Entry Fees (Belt Challenges)
- **Rookie Belts:** 50-100 coins
- **Category Belts:** 100-250 coins
- **Championship Belts:** 250-500 coins
- **Tournament Belts:** Varies by tournament

### Rewards (Winning)
- **Winner:** 50-100% of entry fee pool
- **Loser:** 10-25% consolation (if applicable)
- **Example:** If entry fee is 100 coins, winner might get 75 coins, loser gets 15 coins

## Transaction History

View all your coin activities:
1. Click "My Coins" from the dropdown menu
2. Scroll to "Transaction History"
3. See all transactions including:
   - Coin purchases
   - Belt challenge entries
   - Rewards earned
   - Admin grants/deductions
   - Refunds

Each transaction shows:
- **Type:** What the transaction was for
- **Amount:** Coins added (+) or deducted (-)
- **Balance After:** Your balance after the transaction
- **Description:** Details about the transaction
- **Date:** When it occurred

## Tips for Managing Coins

1. **Start Small:** Begin with lower-value belt challenges to learn the system
2. **Save for Big Challenges:** Championship belts require more coins but offer bigger rewards
3. **Check Belt Values:** Higher-value belts cost more to challenge but offer better rewards
4. **Watch Your Balance:** Always ensure you have enough coins before challenging
5. **Track Transactions:** Review your history regularly to understand coin flow

## Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient coins" error
- **Solution:** Purchase more coins or wait for rewards from previous challenges

### Coins not appearing after purchase
- **Solution:** Check your transaction history. If purchase shows as "COMPLETED" but coins aren't there, contact support

### Want to refund a purchase?
- **Solution:** Contact support. Refunds are processed manually and coins will be deducted from your account
